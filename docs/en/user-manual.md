# User manual (EN)

## 1. Introduction
This manual explains how to use **Escoles Publiques** step by step, screen by screen.

Profiles:
- `ADM`: full administration
- `USER`: limited dashboard access

## 2. Sign in
What to do:
1. Open the login screen.
2. Enter email and password.
3. Click **Sign in**.

![Login (EN)](/ajuda/codex_images_real/en/login-admin.png)

### 3.1 Top section

### 3.2 Bottom section
What you see:
- scope cards

## 4. Schools
### 4.1 List
What to do:
1. Go to **Schools**.
2. Filter using the search box.
3. Review results and actions.

![Schools list (EN)](/ajuda/codex_images_real/en/escoles.png)

### 4.2 Create school
What to do:
1. Click **New school**.
2. Fill required fields.
3. Save.

![Schools create (EN)](/ajuda/codex_images_real/en/escoles-crear.png)

### 4.3 School details
What to do:
1. Click **Details** on a row.
2. Review full information.

![Schools details (EN)](/ajuda/codex_images_real/en/escoles-detall.png)

### 4.4 Edit school
What to do:
1. Open **Edit**.
2. Change the fields.
3. Save changes.

![Schools edit (EN)](/ajuda/codex_images_real/en/escoles-edit.png)

### 4.5 Delete school
What to do:
1. Click **Delete**.
2. Confirm action.

![Schools delete (EN)](/ajuda/codex_images_real/en/escoles-esborrar.png)
